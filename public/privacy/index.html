Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

VAULT ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your personal and financial information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a securely hashed password.

Onboarding information: Income, expenses, net worth estimates, financial goals, and other inputs you provide during setup. This data is used solely to personalize your experience.

Financial account data (via Plaid): If you choose to connect your bank accounts, we receive read-only access to account balances, transaction history, and account metadata through Plaid Technologies, Inc. We never receive or store your bank login credentials.

Usage data: Anonymized information about how you interact with the app, including features used and session duration. This helps us improve the product.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Calculate and display your Wealth Velocity Score
  • Generate personalized financial moves and recommendations
  • Power the AI Concierge with context about your financial situation
  • Project your financial trajectory and FI (financial independence) date
  • Improve and develop new features
  • Send important service notifications (not marketing, unless you opt in)

3. Third-Party Services

We work with the following trusted partners:

  • Plaid Technologies: Secure bank account connection. Subject to Plaid's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase: Secure database and authentication infrastructure hosted in the United States.
  • Anthropic: AI model provider powering the VAULT Concierge. Financial context sent to Anthropic is not used to train their models.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management. Subject to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

We do not sell your personal or financial data to any third party, ever.

4. Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored encrypted at rest. Financial account data retrieved via Plaid is stored in isolated, access-controlled databases. We follow industry-standard security practices and conduct regular security reviews.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time from the Settings screen within the app, or by contacting us at the address below.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all data
  • Portability: Export your data in the app under Settings → Export Data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate personal information

California residents have additional rights under CCPA. EU/UK residents have additional rights under GDPR. Contact us to exercise any of these rights.

7. Children's Privacy

VAULT is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use of VAULT after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
