## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Every finance app shows you your money. VAULT tells you what to do with it.

Connect your accounts (read-only, via Plaid) and VAULT turns your real financial data into specific money moves — exactly what to do next and why it's worth doing. Not generic tips. Moves drawn from your actual accounts: idle cash that could be earning more, credit utilization quietly hurting your score, a savings or retirement account you're missing, subscription creep worth reviewing.

## Data Safety Section

### Data Collected
| Data Type | Purpose | Required? |
|-----------|---------|-----------|
| Email address | Account creation & login | Yes |
| Name | Personalization | Yes |
| Financial account info (via Plaid, read-only) | Core feature — score, feed, concierge | Yes |
| App usage data | Analytics & improvement | No (can opt out) |
| Crash logs | App stability | No |

### Data NOT Collected
- Payment card numbers (handled by App Store/Play billing)
- Banking credentials (Plaid is read-only; we never see your password)
- Location data
- Contacts
- SMS or call logs
- Precise device location

### Data Sharing
- We do not sell personal data
- Financial data shared only with Plaid (bank connection) and Supabase (secure hosting)
- AI queries processed by Anthropic — not used to train Anthropic's models

### Security
- Data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Data encrypted at rest (AES-256)
- Users can request data deletion at any time: privacy@getvault.app

### Data Deletion
Users may delete their account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Delete Account, or by emailing privacy@getvault.app. Deletion is processed within 30 days.
