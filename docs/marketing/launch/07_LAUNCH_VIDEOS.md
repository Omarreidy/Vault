# VAULT — 30 Launch Videos (Phase 4)

Formats: 9:16 vertical, 15–35s. All app footage = demo account on Plaid Sandbox; every on-screen dollar figure carries a small **"Illustrative"** caption. No faces required. VO = calm human read (founder or hired VO; quality TTS acceptable for text-led cuts). Every caption links `getsvault.com?utm_source={platform}&utm_medium=organic&utm_content=v{NN}`.

Funnel key: **TOFU** = cold reach · **MOFU** = consideration · **BOFU** = close to install/connect.
Platform defaults: TikTok = native text + trending-safe audio bed · Reels = cleaner grade, tighter cut −2s · Shorts = add a 1-line spoken intro (search-driven viewers) and keyword-rich title.
